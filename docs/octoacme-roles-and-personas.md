# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Business Analysts

### Role Summary
Business Analysts elicit requirements, create user stories, map business processes, and clarify business rules. They bridge communication between stakeholders and the technical team to ensure solutions meet business needs.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document business requirements from stakeholders
- Create detailed user stories with acceptance criteria
- Map and analyze business processes and workflows
- Define and clarify business rules and data requirements
- Support requirements traceability and impact analysis
- Facilitate requirements review sessions
- Assist in user acceptance testing (UAT) planning

### Goals
- Ensure solutions align with business objectives
- Maintain clear, testable requirements
- Reduce ambiguity and rework through thorough analysis
- Enable smooth handoffs between business and technical teams

### Typical Communication
- Requirements workshops with stakeholders
- Backlog refinement sessions with Product Manager and Developers
- Documentation via user stories, process diagrams, and business rules
- Collaboration with Project Manager on scope and timeline impacts

---

## UX/UI Designers

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers create user-centered designs, including interfaces, user flows, and wireframes. They ensure solutions are usable, accessible, and aligned with design standards and user needs.

### Responsibilities
- Design user interfaces and interaction patterns
- Create wireframes, mockups, and prototypes
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Ensure accessibility and inclusive design standards
- Develop and maintain design systems and style guides
- Collaborate on user journey mapping
- Provide design specifications to Developers

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, user-friendly experiences
- Ensure design consistency across products
- Meet accessibility standards (WCAG, ADA compliance)
- Validate designs through user feedback and testing

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Manager and stakeholders
- Collaboration with Developers during planning and implementation
- Handoff sessions with detailed design specs and assets
- Usability testing sessions with users and QA

---

## Technical Architects

### Role Summary
Technical Architects define solution architecture, select technology stacks, and establish design patterns. They ensure technical decisions support scalability, maintainability, and alignment with enterprise standards.

### Responsibilities
- Design system architecture and technical solutions
- Select and evaluate technology stacks and frameworks
- Define and enforce coding standards and design patterns
- Review code for architectural compliance and quality
- Identify and mitigate technical risks
- Document architectural decisions and rationale
- Provide technical guidance to development teams

### Goals
- Ensure scalable, maintainable system design
- Balance technical excellence with delivery pragmatism
- Reduce technical debt and architectural complexity
- Enable consistent, high-quality implementation

### Typical Communication
- Architecture review sessions with development teams
- Technical design documentation and ADRs (Architecture Decision Records)
- Consultation with Product Manager and Developers on feasibility
- Risk assessments with Project Manager on technical dependencies

---

## Scrum Masters

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate Agile ceremonies, coach teams in Agile practices, and remove impediments. They foster a culture of continuous improvement and help teams deliver value iteratively.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate Agile ceremonies (standups, planning, reviews, retrospectives)
- Coach team members in Agile principles and practices
- Identify and remove blockers and impediments
- Foster psychological safety and team collaboration
- Track team velocity and health metrics
- Shield team from external distractions
- Drive continuous improvement initiatives

### Goals
- Enable high-performing, self-organizing teams
- Maximize value delivery through iterative processes
- Create a culture of transparency and continuous learning
- Maintain sustainable team pace and morale

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint ceremonies
- One-on-one coaching with team members
- Coordination with Project Manager on progress and risks
- Retrospective facilitation and action item tracking

---

## Support/DevOps Engineers

### Role Summary
Support/DevOps Engineers build and operate CI/CD pipelines, monitor production systems, and ensure reliability. They enable rapid, safe deployments and respond to incidents to maintain service quality.

### Responsibilities
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines and automation
- Monitor production systems and application performance
- Implement infrastructure as code and configuration management
- Conduct incident response and root cause analysis
- Ensure security, compliance, and disaster recovery readiness
- Support release deployments and rollback procedures
- Track and improve system reliability metrics (SLOs, SLAs)

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable, automated deployments
- Maximize system uptime and performance
- Reduce mean time to recovery (MTTR) for incidents
- Build observable, maintainable infrastructure

### Typical Communication
- Release planning and coordination with Developers and QA
- Incident response collaboration across teams
- Status updates on system health and deployments
- Post-mortems and lessons learned documentation
